Tubix - AI-Powered YouTube Team Collaboration Platform

What is Tubix?

Tubix is an AI-powered YouTube content management platform that enables teams to collaborate on video uploads with secure approval workflows.

Why Use Tubix?

Stop sharing your YouTube password with editors. Tubix lets your video editing team upload content for your review and approval before it goes live on your channel.

Key Features of Tubix

  • Secure Role-Based Access - Assign granular permissions to editors, managers, and viewers
  • Video Approval Workflow - Review and approve videos before they publish to YouTube
  • Multiple Channel Management - Manage multiple YouTube channels from one dashboard
  • AI-Powered Tools - Generate optimized titles, descriptions, and tags with AI
  • Team Collaboration - Real-time communication with your video editing team

How Tubix Works

  1. Create a workspace and invite your team
  2. Your editor uploads videos to Tubix (not directly to YouTube)
  3. You receive a notification to review the submission
  4. Approve and Tubix publishes it to your YouTube channel
